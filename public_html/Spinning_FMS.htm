I'm looking to do a cowrite on this song with a great lyricist/singer.


Spinning is a synth-pop track that I started writing about two months ago. I was digging through my old Logic Pro files looking for ideas to write with, and came across a really old vocal melody. The melody I found became the centerpiece of this track. Unfortunately, I wrote out the verse, a break, and a second verse but I'm a bit stumped on where to go from here.

Optimally I'd like to work with a singer & lyricist who has a bit of a piercing voice. A great example is Passion Pit; a vocal which can cut through really thick synthesizers. You can see more details over on the left under Project Needs.

As you can see from my profile, I'm based in Los Angeles. I'm not totally opposed to working with someone outside the area, but I'd definitely like it more if we can meet up in person. If you think you're a good fit, audition and we can get started!

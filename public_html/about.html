FindMySong

Its purpose, its life, its mission!

Gaining a foothold in the music industry is hard. Making it and having a music career is even harder. And getting the big break that we all (secretly) hope for seems nearly impossible.

Maybe you can’t get access to the right means to produce your song; maybe you’re missing that one industry connection for your success or maybe you want to advertise your music, but you don’t have thousands of dollars.

FindMySong is built by musicians in Los Angeles. As musicians ourselves, we have run into many challenges that every now and then left us feeling discouraged, questioning our talent and wishing for someone to be there to understand the situation and help us move forward.

That’s why we created FindMySong - an online platform that allows musicians from all over the world to connect, create music, and revolutionize the music industry.

FindMySong's mission is to give its users not only an opportunity to form mutually beneficial relationships or to collaborate on projects, but it also provides an often much needed support-system.

FindMySong's community understands the struggles you face and supports you on your way to success. Join Findmysong now, create your profile, upload your songs and make new ones with others, work on projects and network with like-minded people and watch fans from all over the world listen to your music, rate it, buy it and love it.
